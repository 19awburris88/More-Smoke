Frequently Asked Questions

How do I track my order?

You can track your order by logging into your account and checking the "Order History" section.

What is your return policy?

We accept returns on unused cigars and merchandise within 14 days of purchase. Contact us for return instructions.

How do I update my subscription?

You can manage your subscription from your account dashboard under the "Membership" section.

Do you offer international shipping?

Currently, we only ship within the U.S. We are working on expanding international shipping options.
